Privacy Policy

Last updated:

BoostBridgeDIY (“we,” “us”) provides a personal-use web app that helps you draft FCRA-aware dispute letters. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

Information we collect

  • Account & Contact: your email address and any messages you send us.
  • Inputs you provide: name, address, DOB format (MM/YYYY or YYYY), last-4 SSN, dispute details you type. These are used only to generate your letters.
  • Usage counts: non-sensitive metadata (how many letters you generated) for quota/plan management.
  • Device/analytics (minimal): basic logs for reliability and abuse prevention.

How we use information

  • To operate the app and generate your letters.
  • To manage plan limits, prevent abuse, and improve reliability.
  • To respond to support requests.

Sharing

We don’t sell personal data. Limited third-party processors (e.g., hosting, payments) may process data only to provide the service and under confidentiality agreements.

Data retention

We keep the minimum necessary for your account, quotas, and support. You can request deletion at any time (see Contact).

Security

We use industry-standard practices to protect data. No method is 100% secure, so please use the app from a trusted device/network.

Your rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children

Not for individuals under 18.

Changes

We may update this policy. We’ll post the new date above.
